About

Kyle’s Background

Kyle Becker is a US-based UX researcher, designer, and strategist. He got his start working at frog Design in the US (Austin) and has since went on to work in several startups, work for frog again in Shanghai, lead a UX team in a local bank in Myanmar undergoing a digital transformation, and work for J.P. Morgan’s blockchain division. He has conducted UX research in the US, El Salvador, Nigeria, Rwanda, Kenya, Indonesia, and Myanmar.

How Kyle Works Today

Today, Kyle works as an individual consultant offering a wide variety of services to clients across digital product strategy, UX design, and UX research.
