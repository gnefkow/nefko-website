This is a good place to end because it makes the abstraction concrete. Emil and Rico are not generic assistants. That suggests a more interesting future than “everyone gets a chatbot.” The more serious pattern is that knowledge workers will operate through small constellations of agents, each acting as a translation layer between a person and some complex technical or organizational system. And because those agents will carry personal habits, local procedures, company data, and operational authority, they will need to be designed and governed as real parts of the organization.
